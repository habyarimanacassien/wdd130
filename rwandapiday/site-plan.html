Rwanda Pi Day Site Plan

Cassien Habyarimana

WDD 130-14

Overview

Purpose

The department of Mathematics at University of Rwanda is celebrating the Pi-day on 14th March 2023. The event will take place in the Camp Kigali where the ceremony will start with Mathematics competition of high school students who excel in Mathematics. Various speches will be delivered by distinguish guests to insipire young generations to love Mathematics and know how and why it is important.

Audience

Welcome to all mathematicians, scientists, and all people who love and honor Mathematics. We especially invite young girls and boys in secondary schools whose sections contain Mathematics as one of the principal courses to attend the event and be inspired by senior Mathematics whose daily life is full of Mathematics and its applications.
